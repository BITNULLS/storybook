Warning!

Before you upload a book, you should:

- Remove all pages that have questions, answers, or answer feedbacks (you will have to add these in manually, later).

- Remove hyperlinks, as they will not be functional when the book is converted to PNG images.

- Make sure all pages are in the order desired.

- If you are uploading a large book, please wait some time. It may take a few seconds up to a minute or two to fully upload

- Only click the upload button once
